# Tester Send Message (copy-paste)

Paste the block below into a message/email when you send the ZIP. Tweak names as
you like. Everything a tester needs is also inside the ZIP
(`README_TESTER.txt` + `BUILD_NOTES.md`).

---

Hey! I've been making a little game called **The World Forgot Us** and I'd love
your help testing an early version. It's a short top-down survival demo — about
**5–10 minutes** — set in a quiet, forgotten wasteland. This isn't the finished
game, it's a rough first playable slice, so the art and sound are still
placeholder. I mainly want to know if it's understandable and fun to poke at.

**How to play it (Windows PC):**
1. Download the zip and **unzip it** somewhere (Desktop is fine).
2. Open the folder and double-click **TheWorldForgotUs_Demo.exe**.
   - Keep the `.exe` and the `.pck` file together in the same folder — the game
     needs both.
3. Windows might show a blue **"Windows protected your PC"** warning because the
   app isn't signed (I'm just one person, not a company). That's expected — click
   **More info → Run anyway**. If your antivirus grumbles, same deal.

**Controls:**
- Move: **WASD** (or arrow keys)
- Interact / search / build: **E**
- Scan: **Q** (right-click)
- Attack: **J** (left-click)
- Pause: **Esc**

**What to do:** head east along the road, search the glowing crates, scan the
fallen radio mast to recover a "memory echo," then head back west to the base
and build the Radio Desk, then rest on the bedroll to save. There's a little
teaser at the end hinting at what's next.

**What I'm after:** just tell me honestly — did it launch OK, did you understand
what to do, did anything break, and was any of it fun/boring/confusing? Bugs and
"I had no idea what to do here" moments are super useful. If it's easy, there's a
short list of questions in `TESTER_FEEDBACK_TEMPLATE.md`, but even a couple of
sentences back is great.

Heads up: it's early — placeholder characters, no real music yet, and the "next
area" is just a hook for now. Thanks so much for trying it! 🙏
